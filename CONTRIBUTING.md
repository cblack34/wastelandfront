# Contributing to Wasteland Front

Thank you for helping make practical guides for Last War: Survival players.

## Core Principles

- **Speed of information first.** Players should find what they need in under 30 seconds.
- **Main Squad First** philosophy is non-negotiable in relevant sections.
- Be honest about mid-game realities (including the Tank squad exception and SSR viability).
- Use short paragraphs, tables, Quick Summary boxes, and bold key takeaways.
- All advice should be validated against current community consensus (r/LastWarMobileGame, active alliances, recent testing).

## Process

1. Create a feature branch from `main`
2. Make focused improvements
3. Open a PR
4. We will request Copilot review where possible and address feedback
5. Squash merge when ready

## What We're Looking For

- Better mid-game hero recommendations with reasoning
- Improved copy-paste alliance messages that have proven effective
- Corrections to outdated mechanics
- Better explanations of confusing systems (gear, skills, research priorities)

## What We're Not Looking For

- Heavy theming or "fluffy" writing
- Late-game whale advice (this site targets new and mid-game players)
- Unverified claims

## Questions?

Open an issue or reach out in the repo discussions.

Let's help players get stronger, faster.
